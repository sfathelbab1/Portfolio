About Me

  • Beginnings

    Greetings! My name is Sarah Fathelbab and I’m part of the 18% of women majoring in Computer Science. Before that, I was a Math major - yes, I was the one calculating derivatives and memorizing just about any set of equations you could think of (captivating, am I right?).

  • Fast Forward

    Fast forward three years now, and I’ve learned how to code in a few languages, I’m in the process of creating my own ecommerce website, and also found myself intrigued to one particular branch of Computer Science - Human Computer Interaction and UX Design. Somethings thatt was pleasantly surprising and refreshing to see; a different side to Computer Science.

  • Epiphany

    I was able to see how my Facebook app came to be, how it came to look, and how each specific feature is catered to make the user’s experience one that is of convenience, memorable, as well as impactful on the day to day basis. Combine all of those together, and one could say I was a happy camper.

  • One Happy Camper

    I’m one who’s been deeply fascinated by her major, an avid learner, one who loves to find any means to learn more about the field I’ve dedicated most of my time to, and how I can contribute to society through technology. So with that, how’s one way we could better each other’s skills? What’s that? A callback? Don’t mind if you did!

  • Fin!
    (For Now)
